Mozart, a Classic

Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart is considered the most distinguished representative of Classicism in the History of Music, also understanding as "classical" that set of archetypal qualities worthy of being imitated, which were proposed as a model in the Renaissance, trying to recover the reigning values of ancient Greece which are fundamentally proportion, harmony, serenity, moderation, balance and order. So does the Music of Mozart.
